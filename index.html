Player 1 always starts first.

Player 1 uses the sign "X".

Player 2 uses the sign "O".

Players take turns to place their signs in the 3x3 grid.

The first player to align three signs horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins the game.

If all nine cells are filled without a winner, the game is a draw.
